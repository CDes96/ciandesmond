about

cian desmond.

I am an editor, director, and producer. I have extensive experience working with award-winning short films, documentaries, corporate video, and music videos. Cutting films and videos since 2009, I picked up awards for editing at youth film festivals, before completing my bachelor’s degree at the National Film School in Dublin. My major specialisation was editing, with a minor in sound design. I received first class honours.

 

I am a quick worker, creative collaborator and keen team-player. I am equally at ease working in a busy office environment, as well working with clients remotely.

 

Drop me an email at cianodesmond@gmail.com
