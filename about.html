About

Cian Desmond is a director, writer, and producer from Cork, Ireland.

He has worked with broadcasters such as RTÉ, TG4 and Al Jazeera.

He is a member of Irish Screen Editors, and Screen Producers Ireland.

He runs the Irish film and television production company Desmond Bros.

He is available for work in drama, factual or commercial work.
