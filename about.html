bio

Cian Desmond is an editor, director, and producer. He has extensive experience working with award-winning short films, documentaries, corporate video, and music videos. Cutting films and videos since 2009, he picked up awards for editing at youth film festivals, before completing his  bachelor’s degree at the National Film School in Dublin. His major specialisation was editing, with a minor in sound design. He received first class honours.
