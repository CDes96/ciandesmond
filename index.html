Motion Graphics

Cian Desmond is a professional motion graphics artist, with experience working with Adobe After Effects and Illustrator. He creates animated explainer videos, flat animation, and all sorts of motion graphic related titles and more. For corporate videos which need motion graphics, Cian Desmond can bring a storytelling flair. An Irish video editor based in Ireland, he has worked remotely with clients all over the world.
